Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 19, 2026

OneScore is designed to be personal and private. We respect your data.

1. Data Collection

Personal Data: We collect minimal data (Email, Name) solely for account management.

Health Data: With your permission, OneScore reads Apple Health data (Steps, Sleep, etc.) to automate tracking. This data is synced to your private secure database. We do not sell or share this data.

2. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data instantly from the app settings (Settings > Profile > Delete Account).
